Junkyard is a simple collection app, it ingeniously harnessed the power of technology by connecting citizens with recyclable waste collectors in real-time, offering levels of convenience, transparency, and accessibility. The platform connects waste producers (citizens, households, offices etc.) with recyclable waste collectors in real time and provides a simple, user-friendly interface to request waste pickup and track the progress of recycling. By using Junkyard, individuals and businesses can reduce their carbon footprint by ensuring that their recyclable materials are properly disposed of and repurposed into new products. Find the nearest recyclable waste collector around you, dispose of your recyclable waste using Junkyard and get paid instantly.
